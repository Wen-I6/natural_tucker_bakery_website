WHAT DO WE SAY TO LET IT COOL BEFORE SLICING?
+ −

We know it's hard to resist diving into a warm, freshly baked loaf, but trust us—good things come to those who wait! Slicing too soon can give you a gummy, sticky surprise inside. It’s not undercooked, just a little too cozy and hasn’t had the chance to ‘chill’ yet!
